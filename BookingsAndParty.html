    Party FAQ

    What does the birthday boy/girl receive?

    The birthday boy/girl receives FULL UNLIMATED access to ALL games! Along with this, the birthday boy/girl may select up to 3 of their friends to also receive unlimted access to the arcade. Please note that we are required by law to ensure we follow PEGI guidelines. Please speak to a member of staff should you have any questions or concerns regarding this.

    Is bringing in birthday food/drink allowed? If so, what do you charge?

    Bringing in your own party food and drink is allowed! There is NO CHARGE for this! We ask that you keep all food and drink away from the arcade games and technology, a designated space will be provided to you. We cannot allow drinks in glass containers, we are happy to provide paper cups for a small charge at the counter or bring your own.

    Is alcohol allowed?

    No. This is due to ensuring the safety of any other younger players who may attend the arcade.
